“Marla Ortega was born and raised in Rensselaer, NY. With her passion for food and cooking from her family background. Ortega began her culinary journey by studying at the prestigious Le Cordon Bleu Culinary school.

 

Chef Marla had various experiences before she founded Illium Cafe in 2010. Her first job was as the executive pastry chef of Michael’s Kitchen in 2006. From there she moved on to be a garde manger chef and executive chef at Mouzon House, Envy Lounge, and Pinehaven Country Club.

 

Ortega has also competed in various culinary competitions, including competitions for the American Culinary Federation (in which she has won one silver and four bronze medals). She has also been named a “Signature Chef” by the Albany Chefs’ Food and Wine Festival, a “Rising Star Chef”  by the Capital District Business Review, and a winner of the Food Network show “Guy’s Grocery Games” in 2014.

 

With her passion and enthusiasm for cooking and food, Chef Ortega will deliver local-fresh products, unparalleled in technique, presentation, and taste to you at Illium Cafe.”
